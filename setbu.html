ISI PULSA BOZ

ISI PULSA BOZ (Inovasi peluang usaha sterilisasi buah menggunakan box ozon) is a tool that sterilizes fruit to reduce the level of bacteria in fruit sold on the side of the highway in order to extend the shelf life of the fruit or prevent the fruit from rotting quickly.
